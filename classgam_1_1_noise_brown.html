Detailed Description

template<class RNG = RNGLinCon>
class gam::NoiseBrown< RNG >

Brown noise.

Brown noise has a power spectrum of 1/f^2. This corresponds to an amplitude spectrum with a -6 dB/octave slope. Here it is produced by integrating white noise. The output value is clipped within a specified interval.

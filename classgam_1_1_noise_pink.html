Detailed Description

template<class RNG = RNGLinCon>
class gam::NoisePink< RNG >

Pink Noise.

Pink noise has a power spectrum of 1/f. This corresponds to an amplitude spectrum with a -3 dB/octave slope. In this implementation, it is produced by summing together 12 octaves of downsampled white noise.

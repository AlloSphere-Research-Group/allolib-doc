Detailed Description

template<class Tv = gam::real, class Tp = gam::real>
class gam::Hilbert< Tv, Tp >

Hilbert transform filter.

This filter converts a real signal into a complex (analytic) signal. Corresponding harmonics of the real and imaginary parts of the output are 90 degrees out of phase. The analytic signal can be used for frequency shifting or determining the amplitude envelope of the input signal. Technically speaking, a Hilbert transform converts a real signal into its harmonic conjugate. The input and output of the Hilbert transform, comprise the real and imaginary components of a complex (analytic) signal.

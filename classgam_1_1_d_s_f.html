Detailed Description

template<class Tv = gam::real, class Td = DomainObserver>
class gam::DSF< Tv, Td >

Discrete summation formula (DSF) oscillator.

This produces a finite sum of harmonics whose amplitudes follow a geometric series. The amplitude of harmonic i is ar^i where 'ar' is called the amplitude ratio. The frequency of harmonic i is (i * fr + 1) where 'fr' is called the frequency ratio. Harmonics run from i=0 (the fundamental) to the maximum specified harmonic.

Detailed Description

template<class T>
class gam::RFFT< T >

One-dimensional real-to-complex fast Fourier transform.

The complex sequence format for forward and inverse transforms is [2r0, r1, i1, ... , r(n/2-1), i(n/2-1), 2r(n/2)]

Detailed Description

template<class RNG = RNGLinCon>
class gam::NoiseViolet< RNG >

Violet noise.

Violet noise has a power spectrum of f^2. This corresponds to an amplitude spectrum with a +6 dB/octave slope. Here it is produced by differentiating white noise.
